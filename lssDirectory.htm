Mission Statement:

Listen Software Solutions will bring machine-to-machine communication to your company. We start by describing the problem your company’s management wants (or must) solve in order to survive and grow in a smart direction. We work to discover the "key data indicators" important to your business and apply smart automation to reduce the amount of data collected manually. Telecommunication will use smart, tablet mobile devices with 4G or Wifi-capabilities. Tablets cost under $200. The mobile applications running on the tablets will be written in B4J, B4A, B4I, MVC, HTML5, and Javascript React. Data will be captured and transacted using web services running on an internet server IIS machine and stored in a Microsoft SQL Server Database. Manipulating the data collected will uncover the best direction of growth and survival for your company, and Listen Software Solutions will be there every step of the way to assist in the process.

Start by collecting Two Variables using 4G

  • Temperature and Identification
  • Vehicle Type and Location
  • Identification and GPS Location
  • Identification and Weight
  • Noise and Vibration
  • Viscosity and Pressure
  • Geo Fencing and Identification

Describe the problem, management wants to solve and build a story. Business people are not technical. Don't worry about how the story will be created. Note, the story tells, what your business wants to build. Let us, figure out the "how" the data collection system will be built. As managers, you know the two variables to collect which matter too your organization!

Value Added:: Listen Software Solutions will gather the IOT (expand acronym) data into a hosted database. You don't need to worry about the pain of gathering the data, setting up the database or the code to talk with the devices. We do the heavy lifting. Once the data is in the database server, it can be replicated to your server, or JSON files can be sent to you. We will provide the means via replication or other methodologies, for you to store your own data. You provide the definition of the product to be built. One of the biggest reasons for failure is building the wrong product--a product that you do not want. We build the product you want to gather the data. We figure out the technical how-to. You tell us what you want for your business. We work to define your objectives and the key indicators that will tell you if you’re reaching your goal and confirm your beliefs about your organization.

Cost Structure:: Our billable hourly rate is $50 an hour, Time and Material. Once production starts, transaction block counts can be purchased for as low as a $10 for 1,000 data transactions. We also provide a portable unit to display Key Indicators and Performance reports with a small access fee.

Why do we love our work: Here is our story. We love to solve hard problems. For over a decade, we have been successful at solving problems that others are only now starting to address. Agriculture is proof that the Internet of Things is the future, and we believe that no successful company will be without it. It is exciting to add value to a company through automated data collection, where it was previously only gathered manually. Our unique, exciting technology allows customers to see their own information visually. We like to make our customers smarter by providing dashboards and visual systems that serve to help them understand their own data.
